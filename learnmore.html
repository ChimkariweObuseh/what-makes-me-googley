HOW I CAME TO MAKING THE WEBSITE:

FIRST IDEAS

When we were first told to make something "Googley" for the OneWeekAtGoogle program, I was so lost. I didn't know what it meant. I mean I probably still don't completely know what it means, but at the time, I was so confused, I was literally just going to send a 500 word essay and be done (Side note: This doesn't count as an essay, right? Because I'm WAY over 500 words). However, after a little talk with a relative, I knew I could do more. Much more. I didn't expect a full-website, but I knew it wasn't the end.

BEING GOOGLEY

So first I started researching what it truly meant to be Googley. I looked at all the links, and Googled a bit more. After reading it all, I thought, "Huh, I actually am Googley!" I just needed to find a way to show it.

After a while, it clicked - I knew what I was going to make. It would be one of my biggest projects so far. I didn't know if I was ready, after all the years of learning on my own. It was the best idea I'd had... I was going to make a website!

MAKING THE WEBSITE

At first it felt a bit extreme, but I suddenly realised how perfect it was. It would show that I'm keeping an eye on the goals, striving for excellence, going the extra mile and that I have a really good sense of humour (all things that make you Googley). It was hard at first, but I learned SO MUCH during the time of making the website.

CONCLUDING COMMENT FROM ME:

CONCLUSION

So, that's it! I hope this website showed you that I'm a Googley person. I tried really hard to make this incredible, and I've been working on it for about a week, maybe a bit more. It really was a lot of fun, and I hope you enjoyed reading it as much as I enjoyed making it!
