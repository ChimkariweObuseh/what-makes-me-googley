My Games!

PLEASE NOTE:

As of the time of making this website, all of my games have been made using Microsoft Arcade Makecode. (Huh, it would be super cool if Google had something like this...)

SOMETHING TO REMEMBER:

While playing my games, please recognise that I was completely self taught and learned simply due to interest. (Basically, I was just having fun)

INSTRUCTIONS:

FOR COMPUTERS: Use the WASD keys to move, Z key for A button and X key for B button.

FOR MOBILE: Use the on-screen buttons.

SKELETON SCRAMBLE

This was the very first game I ever made. It doesn't look like much, but it was the game that introduced me to the world of coding! I'm very grateful for that. The game has no story, is pretty confusing, and has some seriously unbalanced gameplay, but, uh, have fun!

SEA SALT ICE CREAM

I made this game for my siblings, because we're all huge fans of Square Enix's Kingdom Hearts. The gameplay is not tremendous, the instructions are unneccessarily confusing, and the characters seem really rushed, but as for the time, this was easily some of the best pixel art I'd ever made.

RECREATION

This is a ripoff version of RobTop's Geometry Dash. Again, my version of this game is not incredible, but a lot of dedication and work went into this game in order to making it seem similar to the actual game. Besides, I was just having fun...

CHESS

This was very hard to make. I remember getting help from some YouTube videos for this game, and even now it still doesn't feel perfected. I think I was transitioning from an intermediate programmer to an advanced programmer when I made this. Sometimes I wonder how I sat through making it. But then again... I'm making a website.

RECREATION: LEVEL 2

Wait, I made a second one? Yes, yes I did. I named this level "Path of Pain", probably just because I'm really bad at naming things. It's way harder than the first level, to the point that the gameplay just irritates people, so, I don't really talk about this one very much.

SICKLE CELL AWARENESS DAY PROJECT

This is when I started going big. I made this game for Sickle Cell Awareness Day 2023 and brought it to my local hospital on the day. It was a huge success, and really got me thinking about Game Developement or Computer Science as a whole for a career.

GET WELL SOON

OK, I know what you're going to say. This isn't actually a game. And you're right. It just looks so good though, so I simply HAD to add it in. Seriously though, just show that to someone who's not feeling well, click, and they're all better.

DINOSAUR TRIALS

And, this was it. I got an Honourable Mention in Microsoft's 8th Arcade Makecode Prehistoric Game Jam, with my submission of Dinosaur Trials. This game is based around the Google Chrome Dinosaur Game, and provides a made-up story and explanation behind it. I think you'll like this one!

So, this was the very first game series I ever made. It's also my only game series so far. The series has a lot of story, and you can slowly see me getting better at coding as you progress through the series.
